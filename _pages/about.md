Welcome to my website! I am a doctoral researcher at Leipzig University. My research focuses on environmental economics, institutional analysis and development, and environmental justice, particularly in the urban air quality monitoring and policy contexts.

Doctoral Appointment
======
In September 2026, I joined the Economics of Connected Natural Commons (ECO-N) doctoral research training group and the Institute for Empirical Economic Research at Leipzig University in Germany.

You can read more about the ECO-N training group [here](https://www.uni-leipzig.de/en/economics-of-connected-natural-commons) and the ICP Urban Air projects [here](https://www.uni-leipzig.de/en/economics-of-connected-natural-commons/research-profile/project).

Previous Affiliations
======
I completed a Master of Science in Sustainability, with Distinction, and a Graduate Certificate in Environmental and Sustainability Economics at Arizona State University’s [School of Sustainability](https://schoolofsustainability.asu.edu/). My former M.S. supervisor is Dr. Danae Hernandez-Cortes. You can discover her research, teaching, and ongoing projects on [her website](https://hernandezcortes.github.io/).

I completed a Bachelor of Arts in Global Studies, with a minor in Spanish Language, with Honors, at Arizona State University’s [Barrett, the Honors College,](https://barretthonors.asu.edu/) and the [School of Politics and Global Studies](https://spgs.asu.edu/). My former Honors thesis supervisor is Dr. Glenn Sheriff. You can explore his work and teaching [here](https://search.asu.edu/profile/3147817).
